how to make french friest

this recipe is very basic and basically doesn't need much ingredients or effort , it's simple and have a satisfying taste

ingredients

  • potatoes
  • oil
  • salt
  • sauce

How to make

  1. peel your potatoes
  2. cut it to fingers shape
  3. salt it
  4. pre heat the oil till 50C
  5. put in your potatoes
  6. let it season for 15 min
  7. put a towel on a plate and take your potatoes off the oil with a spoon
  8. add your sauce to the plate and enjoy!
